Scale of Numbers

People think of Infinity as a large number. We generally think of what is called "Absolute Infinity" as the largest number. But there are many levels of infinity. 1 Trillion is as far as most people think of, but the scale of numbers is fascinating

Number Name Description
a -> a + 1 Succession The next number after a
a -> a + b Addition Adding b to a
a -> a * b Multiplication Multiplying a by b
a -> a ^ b Exponentiation Raising a to the power of b
a -> a ↑↑ b Tetration Iterated exponentiation
a -> a ↑↑↑ b Pentation Iterated tetration
a -> a ↑↑↑↑ b Hexation Iterated pentation
a -> a ↑...↑ b, 63 arrows Graham's Number The largest number used in a mathematical proof is g63
TREE(3) TREE(3) A fast-growing heirarchal function
Meameamealokkapoowa oompa Meameamealokkapoowa oompa A number of Googology
RAYO() Rayo's Number The largest number ever defined
Uncomputables Uncomputable Uncomputable numbers are numbers that cannot be computed, even by Turing machines

Scale of Numbers is a project by Zander Lewis.
